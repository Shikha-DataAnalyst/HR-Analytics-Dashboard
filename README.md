# HR-Analytics-Dashboard

## Objective:
Analyze employee attrition, satisfaction and workforce trends using Excel dashboard.

## Tools Used:
- Excel
- Pivot Tables
- Charts
- Slicers

## KPIs:
- Attrition Rate
- Attrition Count
- Total Employee Count
- Active Employee Count
- Average Employee's Age
  
## Key Insights:
- Highest attrition observed in R & D department
- Employees aged 25–34 showed maximum attrition
- Job satisfaction levels revealed clear patterns across roles and departments

## Business Recommendations:
- Strengthen employee engagement and career growth initiatives in high-attrition departments
- Introduce retention and mentorship programs for employees in the 25–34 age group
- Conduct regular feedback surveys to identify workplace concerns early
